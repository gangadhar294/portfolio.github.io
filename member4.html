Port Logistics

CargoMax Optimizer

The Problem: Ships arrive with mixed cargo: Light/Expensive (Electronics) vs Heavy/Cheap (Raw Material). The port has a fixed weight limit.

Solution: We used 0/1 Knapsack (DP) to mathematically select the perfect subset of containers.

Unlike a Greedy approach, DP evaluates every possible combination to maximize profit without breaking the weight limit.

Last Mile Delivery

SwiftLink Delivery

The Problem: Delivery trucks were wasting fuel by driving random routes between the University, Market, and Residential zones.

Solution: Modeled as the Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP). We calculate the Hamiltonian Cycle with minimum weight.

For our city clusters, we use the Held-Karp algorithm (DP + Bitmasking) to find the exact optimal route.
