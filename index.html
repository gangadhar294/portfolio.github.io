Concept 01

Algorithmic Efficiency

Algorithm efficiency measures how effectively an algorithm utilizes computational resources, primarily time and memory, to solve a problem.

Ex: Merge Sort , Bubble Sort , FFT

Concept 02

Time & Space Complexity

Measures used to describe the efficiency of an algorithm, indicating how its resource requirements.

Ex: O(1) [Hash Map] , O(N log N) [Quick Sort]
