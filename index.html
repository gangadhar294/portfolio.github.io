Concept 01

Algorithmic Efficiency

Algorithm efficiency measures how effectively an algorithm utilizes computational resources, primarily time and memory, to solve a problem. It is a critical concept in computer science.

Ex: Merge Sort , Bubble Sort , FFT

Concept 02

Time & Space Complexity

Measures used to describe the efficiency of an algorithm, indicating how its resource requirements scale as the size of the input data increases.

Ex: O(1) [Hash Map] , O(N log N) [Quick Sort]

Concept 03

Importance of Algorithms

Algorithms are the fundamental logic that drives the modern world. They turn unstructured chaos into organized, actionable intelligence.
