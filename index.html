We designed a smart city framework. We broke down the urban infrastructure into nodes and edges to apply efficient algorithmic solutions. Here are the core modules:

Traffic Optimization

Using Dijkstra's Algorithm to find shortest paths for ambulances and reducing congestion.

Module 02

Water Distribution

Applied Prim's Algorithm (MST) to connect all houses with pipes using minimum wire length.

Zoning & Sorting

Utilized Merge Sort to organize commercial vs residential data efficiently for city planning.
